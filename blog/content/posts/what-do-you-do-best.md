title: What do you do best?
date: 2026-07-21
description: Had a call with a friend recently and it had me thinking
topic: Myself
---

I talked to a friend earlier today and one of the topics of discussion was what do we think we do best? I didn't feel to confident in the answer because truthfully I don't have a proven case to point to. 

I said that I think I'm best at making things that interest me, it doesn't matter so much whether someone finds value in what I make it first has to be of interest to me. I like what I like is what I'm trying to say, that is a way that I want to try and go about things at least. 

I also find it difficult to stick to one long haul project finding that I like to jump between a lot of things and that my motivations to make are largely driven by inspiration and emotion. There's definitely more room for me to have a more structured approach in my life I lean into the spontaneity to much.

I don't know it just had me thinking.
